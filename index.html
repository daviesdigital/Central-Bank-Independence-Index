The Central Bank Independence Index

This project aims to create a data infrastructure for both academics and policymakers. The database contains information on the CBIE index in (Romelli, 2022), as well as the most commonly used indices of central bank independence (see Grilli, Masciandaro and Tabellini, 1991 and Cukierman, Webb and Neyapti, 1992, among others) for 154 countries between 1972 and 2017. The data draw on central bank legislations obtained from central bank websites and other sources.
